Welcome to Career Club!

What you are thinking might not be what you THINK you are thinking.

Join our club today to speak with adults and college students and learn about their experience in their field!

Our meetings are every Monday during lunch in room 26.

Goal:

Our club is dedicated to providing exposure to students about different career paths in advance of the college application process.

Mission:

We bring in speakers with experience in various career fields to talk about their career paths from when they were in school to where they are now, challenges they faced, and a typical day in the life of their occupation.
